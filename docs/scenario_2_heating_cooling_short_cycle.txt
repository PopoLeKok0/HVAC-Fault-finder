Scenario 2: Energy Wasting Faults
---------------------------------
Purpose: Demonstrate the system identifying physical mechanical overlaps and control failures.

Key Characteristics:
- Heating and Cooling Valves open at the same time around timestamps 10-30.
- Fan is toggling on/off rapidly around timestamps 40-60.
- The unit runs the fan while the schedule commands UNOCCUPIED.

Expected Results from Application:
- Simultaneous Heating and Cooling
- Short Cycling
- Schedule Mismatch
