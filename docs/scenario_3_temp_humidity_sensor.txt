Scenario 3: Comfort and Sensor Faults
-------------------------------------
Purpose: Demonstrate the system identifying environmental deviations caused by mechanical or sensor degradation.

Key Characteristics:
- Supply Air Temperature is consistently ~2.5 degrees above setpoint.
- Return Air Humidity spikes to ~75%.
- Zone Office 2 sensor flatlines completely, indicating a dead sensor.

Expected Results from Application:
- Supply Temperature Out of Range
- High Humidity Levels
- Zone Sensor Failure
